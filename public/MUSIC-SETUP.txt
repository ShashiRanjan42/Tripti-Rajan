# Wedding Music Setup

To enable background music on the wedding website:

1. Add your wedding music file to this folder named: `wedding-music.mp3`
   Example: `public/wedding-music.mp3`

2. The audio player is already configured to:
   - Auto-play at 40% volume when the user taps the splash screen
   - Loop the music throughout the session
   - Allow pause/resume via the floating music button (top-right)

3. Recommended free royalty-free Indian wedding music sources:
   - Pixabay (https://pixabay.com/music/search/indian%20wedding/)
   - Free Music Archive
   - Bensound

4. To add family/couple photos, place images in the /public folder and
   update the PersonPhoto components in src/routes/index.tsx
   (replace the initial-letter placeholders with <img> tags)

5. Family photo: place your photo at /public/family-photo.jpg
   Then update the family photo section in src/routes/index.tsx to use:
   <img src="/family-photo.jpg" alt="Family" />
